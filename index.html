ABOUT US

The story behind our homemade tiffin service

Not all hunger is the same. Sometimes, you don't just miss food; you miss home. The familiar smell from the kitchen. The warmth of a simple meal. The comfort of knowing someone cooked it just for you.

That's why we're on a mission to bring you honest, homemade, healthy food. This isn't restaurant food. It isn't fast food. It's food made with the same care and love we cook for our own families.

And wherever you are, we hope every dabba we send reminds you that someone's thinking of you.
